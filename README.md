## Key Findings
1. **High-Value Revenue Exposure** 
48.6% of total revenue is generated by segments currently at risk of churn. This identifies a critical financial vulnerability where nearly half of the company's "top line" depends on improving retention.

2. **Contractual Instability** 
Monthly contracts exhibit a 100% churn rate, whereas Quarterly and Annual plans form the stable core of the business. This highlights the subscription model itself as a primary risk factor.

3. **Operational Friction (The Leading Indicator)** 
Feature Importance analysis (Score: 0.47) isolates Support Calls as the single most predictive driver of churn. This proves that customers aren't leaving due to price, but due to unresolved service-related friction.
